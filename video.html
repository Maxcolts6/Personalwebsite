My love for photography

My love for photography started at a really young age when I was about 12. I enjoyed trying to film lacrosse games, ski trips, and family vacations. As I got older I began to focus more on locations to take photos. I like to travel and explore new areas and my photos show that!

Favorites and why

  1. National Parks
    • There are a variety of features
    • They have recognizable features
    • They often have vibrant outdoor colors
  2. Skiing
    • I love to ski
    • I think that the white snow looks so good next to a skier
    • They have really good action shots
  3. Sports
    • I have always loved sports
    • There is a chance that you can capture a really cool moment
    • You can follow your favorite team and players
